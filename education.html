Teaching and tutorials

Courses, student mentoring and open tutorials. Most of the tutorial material is taken from other sources and adapted to my needs. If there is some attribution I have missed, let me know.

Courses taught

  • Bioinformatics applied to transcriptomics analysis — MSc in Neurobiology, UNAM. Guest lecturer (2024); coordinator and professor (2025, 2026).
    • Spatial transcriptomics tutorials — introduction to spatial sequencing (link), spot deconvolution (link), imaging-based spatial transcriptomics / MERFISH (link) and spatial transcriptomics class 12 (link).
  • Basic neurobiology course: cellular neurobiology and neurodevelopment — INB-UNAM graduate program (2024, 2025), guest lecturer.
  • Developmental Biology — Graduate program in Biochemical Sciences, UNAM (2024), guest lecturer.
  • Bioinformatics Week 2024 (Semana de Bioinformática) — INB-LAVIS, organizer and instructor (40 h).
    • Bulk RNA-seq tutorial — differential expression analysis of RNA-seq in R. link
  • 3rd Summer Retreat on Bioinformatics and Complex Networks (2024, Mineral de Pozos) — workshop speaker (single-cell transcriptomics).
    • Single-cell RNA-seq tutorial — single-cell RNA-seq analysis in R. link
  • Teaching extended to undergraduates of the Genomic Sciences degree (LCG) at ENES Juriquilla.
